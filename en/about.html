ABOUT US

FRUTON LLC is one of the biggest suppliers of juice concentrates and purees on CIS market.

Since 2008 we represent the biggest Israeli company producing raw materials for juice products and child nutrition - Gan Shmuel Foods Group. Shipments are carried out from two plants located in Israel and one production site in Europe (the Netherlands).

In addition to Gan Shmuel Foods Group, products, FRUTON LLC supplies raw materials for production of juices, nectars, fruit drinks and child nutrition from the other parts of the world: America, Africa, Europe, Asia. Our partners are well-known factories and agricultural companies.

Geography of our deliveries now covers all major markets of former USSR, among them Russia, Belarus, Kazakhstan, Ukraine, Azerbaijan, Uzbekistan, Armenia, Lithuania, Georgia.

The second direction of our activity is packaging solutions.

FRUTON LLC is a sole representative of ARAN Packaging, company of CIS market. The company produces aseptic and non-aseptic bags, which are used for packing and storage of a wide range of food products: from fruit, vegetable, berry juices and purees to wines and fruit preparation for the confectionery and dairy industry. Nowadays ARAN Packaging implements shipments from three factories located in Israel, Spain and USA.

    In order to provide reliable service and the best prices for our partners, we:
  • • cooperate directly with the manufacturers;
  • • have direct contracts with sea lines, railway and auto-carriers;
  • • offer direct deliveries and deliveries from our warehouses in Novorossiysk, Moscow, Saint-Petersburg and Riga;
  • • are constantly working on logistics optimization;
  • • approach the needs of each customer individually.
